# Benchmark formating regex matchers and replacements

## align benchmarks in the range of [-16pi, ..., 16pi]
Match:   `, (16, 1), (.*>>)`
Replace: `, $1, $2 `

## align other benchmarks benchmarks
Match:   `, ([0-9]),`
Replace: `,  $1`

## end of line alignment
Match:   ` ([0-9]+)$`
Replace: `$1`
